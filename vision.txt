PROJECT: AI Fashion Color Fit Matcher

PROBLEM:
Many people don't know which clothing colors suit their skin tone.
This leads to confusion while shopping and choosing outfits that 
don't actually look good on them.

5 FEATURES:
1. Skin Tone Detector - detects warm/cool/neutral tone from photo
2. Color Recommender - suggests best colors based on skin tone
3. Outfit Suggester - recommends full outfits for occasions/seasons
4. AI Chatbot Stylist - answers fashion questions like a real stylist
5. Virtual Color Try-On - preview how a color looks before buying

USER FLOW:
User opens app -> Uploads photo -> App detects skin tone -> 
Shows recommended colors -> Suggests outfits -> 
User chats with AI stylist for more advice -> 
Result: User knows what colors/outfits suit them
